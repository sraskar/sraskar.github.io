Siddhisanket Raskar

I am a post-doctoral researcher in the Data Sciences group with theArgonne Leadership Computing Facility(ALCF) at Argonne National Laboratory. I work on evaluating the efficacy of AI architectures for scientific machine learning and on the design of next-generation AI architectures for science

I obtained my Ph.D. under the guidance of Guang R. Gao on the topic of "Dataflow Software Pipelining for Codelet Model using hardware-software co-design” from Electrical and Computer Engineering department at the University of Delaware. I also have a Master’s in Computer Science from the University of Delaware and a Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering from the University of Pune, India.

I am interested in pursuing a research-oriented career in the field of computer architecture, high-performance computing, and machine learning.

Research Interests :

  • Computing and Dataflow Models
  • Computer Architecture and Systems
  • Compilers Technology and Runtime Systems
  • Program mapping and optimization under dataflow models
